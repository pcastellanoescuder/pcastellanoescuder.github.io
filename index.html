Biography

I obtained my Bachelor degree in Biology at the University of Barcelona (UB) in June 2016. In July 2017 I obtained my Master’s degree in Bioinformatics and Biostatistics at the University of Barcelona (UB) and the Open University of Catalonia (UOC). My master thesis was titled “Prediction model of response to a certain treatment in childhood obesity”.

During my bachelor and master degree studies, I focused my research in the search of epigenetic and metabolomic biomarkers in Childhood obesity.

Currently, I’m a PhD student in the group of “Biomarkers and Nutritional & Food Metabolomics” and also in the group of “Statistics and Bioinformatics” at the University of Barcelona. My research is focused on food metabolomics.
