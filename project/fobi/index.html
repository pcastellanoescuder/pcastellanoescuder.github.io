FOBI: an ontology to represent food intake data and associate it with metabolomic data

Nutrition research can be conducted by using two complementary approaches: (i) traditional self-reporting methods or (ii) via metabolomics techniques to analyze food intake biomarkers in biofluids. However, the complexity and heterogeneity of these two very different types of data often hinder their analysis and integration.
Pol Castellano Escuder, Raúl González-Domínguez, David S. Wishart, Cristina Andrés-Lacueva, Alex Sánchez-Pla
